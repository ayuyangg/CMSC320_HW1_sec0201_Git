Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: We use Git for version control, since it allows users to experiment with code changes.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to the field of healthcare. For example, doctors could analyze the different body weights of patients over time to determine the effect of treatments on their weight and other factors.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging preserves the complete history of both branches, while rebasing rewrites the project history.
